Today, short urls are dumb :(

When you want to share content on a webpage, you create a short url. This url brings you to the current page... not the content you were originally trying to share.

We found a solution. Download our chrome extension, and we will bring you to the content you were actually trying to share in the first place.

or

NOTE:This is only an alpha and proof of concept. It was built over 12 hours at a hackathon hosted by Intrepid Labs in Cambridge, MA. We acknowledge that there are bugs and missing features, but we just wanted to show you what is possible. Currently Chrome is the only supported browser. We hope you enjoy!
